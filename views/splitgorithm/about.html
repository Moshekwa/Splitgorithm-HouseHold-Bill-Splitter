What is Splitgorithm?

Splitgorithm is an app which helps households to balance their financial responsibilities at just a click of a button.
It does this by dividing each expense equally amongst members of the household in question.

How does it work?

One member of the household can create a group for the household.
Once the group is created, other members can be invited to join the group via email or they can join on the app using the group name.
Any member can post an expense that they have paid for.
The app will then determine what other members owe to the member who posted the expense.

Why Splitgorithm?

One of the leading causes of conflicts in households are expenses related.
These can be avoided using the Splitgorithm app.
Using our app every member of the household knows:

  • Which expense they are responsible for
  • Which expenses are the other members responsible for
  • How much is owed to them at all times
